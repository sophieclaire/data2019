Quality of Life Index around the World in 2019

by Sophie Stiekema, 10992499; Data retrieved from Numbeo

The Quality of Life Index measures overall quality of life by taking into account several other variables: purchasing power, safety, health care, cost of living, property price to income ratio, traffic commute time, pollution and climate.
Denmark currently holds the highest Index, while the Netherlands ranks 6th.
